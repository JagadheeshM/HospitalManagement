Services

  1. Emergency Department (ED): Provides immediate medical care to patients experiencing acute illnesses, injuries, or emergencies.
  2. Medical/Surgical Department: Handles general medical conditions and provides non-surgical treatments for various diseases and injuries.
  3. Intensive Care Unit (ICU): Specialized unit that provides critical care for patients with severe illnesses or injuries, requiring constant monitoring and advanced medical interventions.
  4. Obstetrics and Gynecology Department (OB/GYN): Focuses on women's reproductive health, offering prenatal care, childbirth services, gynecological examinations, and treatment for reproductive system disorders.
  5. Pediatrics Department: Specializes in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents, providing routine check-ups, vaccinations, treatment for childhood illnesses, and pediatric consultations.
  6. Neurology Department: Focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the nervous system, including neurological examinations, brain imaging, and treatment of neurological disorders.
  7. Cardiology Department: Concentrates on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart and cardiovascular conditions, including cardiology consultations, cardiac testing, and interventional procedures.
  8. Oncology Department: Specializes in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer, providing medical and radiation oncology services, chemotherapy, and cancer-related support.
